About

Brand direction

What this is

A premium combat gear concept. Limited production, dark presentation, and a coherent object language across training essentials, protective pieces, conditioning tools and ceremonial outerwear.

How it's structured

Products are grouped into four distinct series rather than treated as disconnected inventory. Each series has its own visual tone and product logic. The catalog is built from within that structure.
